the problem

The hardest part of frontier work
isn't the math. It's the silence.

Push far enough into a question and you're the only person nearby who cares about exactly this. So you chase it alone.

why now

Three things just became true.

  • AI Researchers run AI sessions daily — a live signal of what they're working on.
