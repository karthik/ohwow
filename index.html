What it feels like

From a blinking cursor to a room full of people who get it.

You were deep in a problem nobody around you is working on. That’s the loneliness of frontier work — being the only person in the building who cares about exactly this.

ohwow listens to the meaning of your session, not the keywords, and finds the others.

  • Semantic match, not tag soup — embeddings over your real reasoning.
  • ORCID-anchored, so the person on the other side is real.
  • Ephemeral rooms — conversations, not another inbox.

ohwow exists to end that silence — and put the wonder back in. ✦
