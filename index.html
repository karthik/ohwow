Set up once · two minutes

Install it, point it at your ORCID, and forget about it.

It lives inside the tools you already use. After setup, the whole thing is a single word.

1
INSTALL

Add the skill to Claude

Pull the ohwow skill from our server. One line, and /wonder shows up in every session you run.

# add the skill
/plugin install wonder@ohwow.science
2
CONFIGURE

Set your ORCID once

Save your ORCID iD to local config. It travels with each request so the people you meet are real researchers, and it stays on your machine.

/wonder config orcid 0000-0002-…
3
WONDER

Run /wonder when it gets quiet

It summarizes the session, sends the summary with your ORCID, and drops you into a room of people working on the same thing.

/wonder
→ room: protein-folding · low-data
# 7 inside
~/research — ohwow
What happens

A blinking cursor, then a room of people who get it.

You had been deep in something nobody around you works on. Run /wonder and the screen fills with people who do.

It matches on what the session was about, not the words you happened to type.

  • It reads your reasoning and matches on meaning, not tags.
  • Everyone is anchored to a real ORCID, so the person across from you is too.
  • Rooms close when the conversation does. There is no inbox to clear.
